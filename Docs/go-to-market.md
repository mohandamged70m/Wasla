# Wasla — Go-to-Market & Growth Strategy

---

## 1. Market Positioning

**Brand Voice:** Friendly, capable, and local. We speak to the Egyptian entrepreneur who runs their business from their phone.

**The One-Liner:**
> Wasla is the WhatsApp operations hub for Egyptian businesses — one shared inbox for your team, smart order/appointment tracking, and automated follow-ups. All inside WhatsApp.

**Differentiation:**
- Built for Egypt (Arabic-first, EGP pricing, local support)
- Horizontal (works for e-commerce, clinics, service businesses)
- Affordable (starts at 299 EGP/month vs. international competitors at $50+/mo)
- Fast onboarding (connect WhatsApp in 2 minutes)

## 3. First 100 Customers Plan (Days 1–30)

### Tactic 1: Instagram DM Outreach (Target: 40 customers)

**Who:** Instagram profile that:
- Has a WhatsApp number in bio
- Posts products/services regularly
- Has 1K-50K followers
- Egyptian-based

**Script (Arabic):**
> السلام عليكم 🫡
> بشوف إن نشاطك التجاري شغال كويس على الانستجرام! أنا صانع تطبيق اسمه "Wasla" بيسمح ليك تدير محادثات الواتساب مع فريقك — يعني لو عندك مبيعات كتير على الواتساب، تقدر تتابع الطلبات وتخصص ردود وتشوف أداء فريقك كله في شاشة واحدة.
> جرب مجاناً 7 أيام. حابب تجرب؟

**Volume:** 20 DMs/day. Personalize each (reference their products). Track replies in a spreadsheet.

### Tactic 2: Facebook Group Engagement (Target: 25 customers)

**Groups to join:**
- بزنس أونلاين مصر
- التجارة الإلكترونية في مصر
- سيدات الأعمال المصريات
- أصحاب المشاريع الصغيرة مصر
- كلينيك مصر (for clinic owners)

**Strategy:**
- Week 1: Just answer questions. No promotion. Build reputation.
- Week 2: Share value posts ("5 ways to never lose a WhatsApp customer again")
- Week 3: Soft pitch in comments when relevant ("I built a tool for this exact problem — happy to show you")

### Tactic 3: Referral Program (Target: 15 customers)

- First 50 customers: Refer 1 friend → get 1 month free
- Refer 3 friends → get 3 months free
- Refer 5 friends → get 6 months free

### Tactic 4: Content SEO (Target: 10 customers)

**Target keywords (Arabic):**
- إدارة طلبات الواتساب
- تنظيم محادثات الواتساب للبزنس
- برنامج إدارة الواتساب للفريق
- ازاي أتابع طلبات الزبائن على الواتساب
- أفضل تطبيق لإدارة الواتساب للشركات

**Content:** Blog posts + YouTube videos for each keyword.

### Tactic 5: Delivery Company Partnerships (Target: 5 customers)

**Approach:** Partner with 3-5 delivery companies in Cairo/Alex.
**Value prop:** "Recommend Wasla to your merchants and they'll stop calling you about delivery status — all updates go through WhatsApp automatically."
**Revenue share:** 15% commission on referred customer's first 3 months.

### Tactic 6: Direct Cold Outreach (Target: 5 customers)

Using EGYPTIAN BUSINESS DIRECTORY, collect WhatsApp numbers of SMEs. Send personalized voice note via WhatsApp introducing Wasla.

## 8. Competitive Response Plan

**If Kommo enters Egypt with aggressive pricing:**
- Double down on Arabic-first experience (RTL, Egyptian slang in onboarding)
- Offer local phone + WhatsApp support (they won't)
- Build community (they're a faceless international company)

**If WhatsApp Business app adds team features:**
- Be deeper (CRM pipeline, analytics, integrations)
- Be faster (they move slowly — we ship weekly)
- Be cheaper for teams (WhatsApp charges per device — we don't)

**If Gallabox/Interakt launches in Egypt:**
- Move faster on local integrations (Fawry, Paymob, Bosta, Aramex)
- Local content marketing (they can't write in Egyptian Arabic)
- Door-to-door for clinics (they won't do feet on the street)
